Jurassic Left Behind - Glossary

character

Jophery Brown
Also: Jophery, Brown
The InGen worker killed during the raptor transfer incident. His old badge becomes a dangerous second identity after the park's rollback erases Mateo.
First appears in: chapter-001
Mateo Valverde
Also: Mateo, Valverde
A temporary sanitation and biohazard contractor brought to Isla Nublar after Jophery Brown's death. His temporary badge works until Nedry's shutdown forces the park into a backup access table that does not know he exists.
First appears in: chapter-002
Rosa
Also: Rosa Mendez
An east transport worker left injured in the visitor center service corridors. She becomes Mateo's witness, route partner, and the first person who keeps saying his real name after the system drops it.
First appears in: chapter-014

access-control

D-17
Also: reader D-17
A bad raptor-service reader Jophery reported before his death. It misreports identities during fallback and becomes proof that InGen knew the access system could erase workers.
First appears in: chapter-009
Safe-mode rollback
Also: backup reset, access rollback
The park security system's degraded fallback state after Nedry's shutdown. It brings door control back online from an older backup and silently drops newer temporary personnel from the live credential table.
First appears in: chapter-005
Temporary badge
Also: temp badge, Mateo's badge
Mateo's legitimate InGen credential, issued for a short-term cleanup contract. It opens staff doors during normal operation but fails after the safe-mode reboot restores an older credential backup.
First appears in: chapter-002

location

East Dock
Also: dock, evacuation boat
The evacuation route Mateo is trying to reach when the rollback traps him behind staff-only doors long enough for the boat to leave.
First appears in: chapter-004
North Dock
Also: maintenance launch, north maintenance dock
A staff and maintenance launch point outside the guest evacuation routes. It becomes Mateo's final escape objective after the east boat leaves.
First appears in: chapter-021
Service corridors
Also: utility corridors, staff corridors
The maintenance routes, locker-room halls, biohazard rooms, laundry paths, and dock-side service spaces that keep Jurassic Park running out of sight.
First appears in: chapter-003
Utility Level B
Also: Service Spine B, utility spine
The concrete service layer beneath guest-facing park buildings, carrying pumps, drains, cable trays, animal-service readers, and maintenance shortcuts that let Mateo move through the park's hidden underside.
First appears in: chapter-012
